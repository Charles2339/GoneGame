# Gone: Endless Runner - Controls & Tips

## 🎮 CONTROLS

### Mobile (Touch Screen)
- **TAP** - Jump
- **DOUBLE TAP** - Double Jump (in air)
- **SWIPE DOWN** - Slide

### Keyboard
- **SPACE** - Jump
- **DOUBLE PRESS SPACE** - Double Jump (in air)
- **S** - Slide

## 🎯 TIPS & TRICKS

### Survival
1. **Double Jump for Distance** - Use double jump to clear large gaps
2. **Slide Under Obstacles** - Crouch slide to pass under high barriers
3. **Use Momentum** - Time your jumps with obstacle patterns
4. **Watch Pacing** - Difficulty increases every 30 seconds

### Scoring
- **Distance**: +10 pts/second (passive)
- **Obstacles Dodged**: +bonus on close calls
- **Power-ups**: Collect for temporary boosts
- **Combo**: Chain dodges for multiplier up to 3.5x

### Power-Ups
- 🟦 **Shield** (Cyan) - Survive one hit damage
- 🟪 **Magnet** (Magenta) - Auto-collect nearby coins
- 🟦 **Slow-Mo** (Blue) - Reduce speed 30% for 5s
- 🟨 **2x Score** (Yellow) - Double score for 10s
